# TikTok

## Live source

URL: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/inspiration/topads/pc/en

No login required. This is TikTok's own official Top Ads library.

How to filter. Set industry to match the moment's category. Set region if the moment is
geo-specific, otherwise leave it broad. Sort by CTR to surface the ads with the strongest
click performance rather than raw impressions.

How to pick Top N. Take the top 10 ads after the CTR sort within the chosen industry.
Open each one and check the retain rate curve, TikTok shows this frame by frame. An ad
with a high CTR but a steep early drop in the retain curve is a bait-and-switch hook and
a poor pattern to copy. Keep the ones where the retain curve stays flat through at least
the first 3 seconds.

How often to refresh. Pull weekly. TikTok's Top Ads list rotates fast, a pull older than
two weeks is treated as stale for the produce station.

What signal to read. CTR rank tells you what got clicked. The frame-by-frame retain curve
tells you what got watched. Use both, a winner needs to score on both axes.

## Starter rules

These rules come from editorial hook-list sources rather than from a live Top Ads pull.
Treat them as a cold-start default. Each is marked with its status.

1. Open on movement or a face within the first half second. Distilled from editorial hook
   lists, to be validated against Top Ads pulls.
2. State the outcome or the tension in the first on-screen text card, before any brand
   name appears. Distilled from editorial hook lists, to be validated against Top Ads
   pulls.
3. Use short 2 to 4 word text cards instead of full sentences, synced to a cut or a beat. Distilled
   from editorial hook lists, to be validated against Top Ads pulls.
4. Keep the first 2 seconds free of logo, price, or CTA. Save the ask for after the hook
   has landed. Distilled from editorial hook lists, to be validated against Top Ads
   pulls.
5. Shoot vertical, handheld, native-feeling footage over polished studio footage. Ads that
   read as a stranger's video outperform ads that read as a commercial. Distilled from
   editorial hook lists, to be validated against Top Ads pulls.
6. Cut on beat roughly every 1 to 2 seconds in the first 5 seconds, then let shots breathe
   longer once attention is won. Distilled from editorial hook lists, to be validated
   against Top Ads pulls.
7. Caption everything. Sound-off viewing is the default assumption for this platform.
   Distilled from editorial hook lists, to be validated against Top Ads pulls.
